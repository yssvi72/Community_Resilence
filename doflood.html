Flood Safety Dos and Don'ts

Do's:

  • Stay informed about weather conditions and flood warnings in your area.
  • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies like water, food, and first aid items.
  • Develop a family emergency plan and practice evacuation routes.
  • Elevate valuable items and electronics in your home to prevent water damage.
  • Turn off utilities (gas, electricity, and water) before evacuating.

Don'ts:

  • Don't walk or drive through floodwaters; it's extremely dangerous.
  • Don't wait until the last minute to evacuate if you're in a flood-prone area.
  • Don't touch electrical equipment if you're wet or standing in water.
  • Don't ignore evacuation orders from local authorities.
  • Don't return to your home until it's declared safe by authorities.

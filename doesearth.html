Earthquake Safety Dos and Don'ts

Do's:

  • Drop, Cover, and Hold On during an earthquake.
  • Stay indoors and away from windows during the shaking.
  • Secure heavy furniture and objects to prevent them from falling.
  • Have an emergency kit with water, food, and first aid supplies.
  • Check for gas leaks and turn off the gas if you smell it.

Don'ts:

  • Don't run outside during the shaking as falling debris can be dangerous.
  • Don't use elevators during or after an earthquake.
  • Don't light matches or candles if there's a gas leak; use a flashlight.
  • Don't block doorways or exits with furniture or objects.
  • Don't return to damaged buildings until they're declared safe.

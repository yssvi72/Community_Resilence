Flood Safety Dos and Don'ts

Do's:

  • Stay informed about weather conditions and tornado warningd in your area.
  • Create an emergency kit with essential supplies like water, food, and first aid items.
  • Develop a family emergency plan and practice evacuation routes.
  • Conduct tornado drills with your family or coworkers to ensure everyone knows what to do when a tornado warning is issued..
  • Turn off utilities (gas, electricity, and water) before evacuating.

Don'ts:

  • Never ignore tornado warnings or assume it won't affect you. Take all warnings seriously..
  • Vehicles are not safe during tornadoes. Abandon your vehicle and seek shelter in a building or a low-lying area if no shelter is available.
  • Opening windows will not equalize pressure and may expose you to flying debris. Focus on taking cover instead.
  • Wait until you receive official all-clear signals or information before leaving your safe shelter..
  • Tornadoes can occur in any regions, not just Tornado Alley. Be prepared regardless of where you live..
